Currently, I am a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Edinburgh funded by the Royal Society univeristy research fellowship working with Dr. Vaishak Belle. Previously, I was a doctoral researcher (2018-2022) at the RWTH-Aachen university, under the supervision of Prof.Gerhard Lakemeyer in the Department of Computer Science. During that time, I was affiliated with the Knowledge-Based Systems Group and the UnRAVeL Research Training Group. I finished my Master in Computer Science (2018) in Nanjing University and Bachelor in Software Engineering (2015) in Central South University.
