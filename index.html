Currently, I am an assistant professor at Nanjing University in the School of Aritificial Intelligence. Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher (2023-2024) at the University of Edinburgh (supervised by Dr. Vaishak Belle) in AIAI. I was a doctoral researcher (2018-2022) at the RWTH-Aachen university (2018-2022) supervised by Prof. Gerhard Lakemeyer. During that time, I was affiliated with the Knowledge-Based Systems Group and the UnRAVeL Research Training Group. I finished my Master in Computer Science (2018) in Nanjing University and Bachelor in Software Engineering (2015) in Central South University.

Research Interest

    Briefly, Artificial Intelligence, Knowledge Representation, Program Verification
    Specifically, I am interested in formalisms of concepts such as knowledge and beliefs, actions and causality, probability, and uncertainty, et al., and reasoning mechanisms with regard to these notions. This covers research areas in AI such as reasoning about actions and causes, reasoning about knowledge and beliefs, and planning and verification for autonomous agents. This also means that most research is theoretical and based on logic and/or formal methods. Please also refer to the latest publication for my current focus.
